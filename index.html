How to play

To start: Enter your and your friends' names and click Start.

Click on a block to pick it up. Mouse wheel or press Q E to rotate a block. Middle click or press Space to turn it ot other side. Click again to place on a board.

First turn must be made in the corner. Latter, newly placed block must touch at least one corner of any your previous blocks. But it must not touch borders of your blocks.

The one who scores most points wins.
